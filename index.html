Goals

What is the purpose of this website?

The purpose of this website is to introduce the user to the Rolling Hills Estates mobile home community.

What are the goals of this website?

The goal of this website is to provide potential customers with information about the homes available for rent or sale at Rolling Hills Estates. This website will also provide current residents with a schedule of community events and alerts as well as employee contact information.

Why will people come to your website? Who is your target audience?

This website will target adults of any age who are interested in an affordable alternative to traditional housing. Also included in the target audience are current members of the Rolling Hills Estates community.

What is the name of this website?

The name of this website will be "Rolling Hills Estates Mobile Home Community".

What do you intend to have this website do? How will your site be different from other existing websites with similar purposes?

Many mobile home communities are owned by a larger real estate trust and subsequently forced to share that organizations website with all the communities that organization owns. This gives the uwer an impoweronal experience and can make navigation complicated. This website will be different because it will focus soley on one mobile home community, providing potential customers with housing information, and existing community members with an easy way to view organized events, alerts, and employee contact information.
